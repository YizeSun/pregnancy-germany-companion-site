Subscriptions and Free Trial

Premium access, free trials, renewals, cancellations, and refunds are managed through Apple. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in the App Store subscription settings.

If a purchase does not unlock Premium, open the app settings and use Restore Purchases. If it still does not work, contact support with the approximate purchase time.

App Features

Pregnancy Germany Companion provides pregnancy planning, weekly context, task organization, health record tracking, glossary terms, official resources, communication templates, case libraries, and export features.

The app is designed for informational organization and Germany-specific preparation. It does not provide diagnosis, treatment, emergency support, or professional medical advice.

Data and Privacy

The app is designed to work without creating an account. Pregnancy dates, preferences, notes, health records, and saved progress are stored locally on your device unless a system feature or Apple service handles them separately.

For details about analytics, subscriptions, device data, and privacy rights, read the Privacy Policy.

Troubleshooting

  • Update to the latest App Store version.
  • Restart the app and your device if screens do not refresh.
  • Check that your selected app language and communication language are set as intended.
  • Use Restore Purchases if Premium access is missing after subscribing.
  • Contact support if exported files, local records, or app content do not appear as expected.

Medical and Emergency Questions

For urgent symptoms, medical concerns, or emergency situations, contact local emergency services or a qualified healthcare professional. The app cannot assess symptoms or replace your doctor, midwife, clinic, or insurer.
