Privacy Policy for Pregnancy Germany Companion

Effective date: July 3, 2026

Our privacy approach

Pregnancy Germany Companion is designed as a local-first pregnancy reference and planning app. You can use the app without creating an account.

We do not sell personal data. We do not use advertising tracking. We do not share pregnancy records, health records, journal entries, appointments, hospitals, addresses, or doctor information with employers, insurers, advertisers, or data brokers.

Data stored on your device

By default, your pregnancy and planning records are stored on your device and are not uploaded to our own servers.

This may include expected due date or pregnancy week, completed tasks, calendar events, prenatal appointments, personal notes, journal entries, symptom logs, health records, local resource choices, hospital or midwife planning information, app language, communication language, appearance settings, and Premium access status.

Apple Health

If you choose to connect Apple Health, the app asks permission to read body weight and sleep data. This data is used only to show your health context inside the app.

The app does not write data to Apple Health. Apple Health data is not sent to TelemetryDeck and is not used for advertising, marketing, or data mining. You can revoke Apple Health permission at any time in the iOS Health app or iOS Settings.

Anonymous analytics

If anonymous analytics is enabled, the app sends privacy-friendly product events to TelemetryDeck so we can understand and improve the app experience.

Events may include app launch, screen views, Premium entry points, purchase or restore events, selected app language, appearance theme, and whether Premium access is active. The app uses an installation-level anonymous identifier for analytics.

Analytics does not include name, email, exact due date, symptoms, journal entries, notes, Apple Health values, hospital, address, doctor, or midwife information. You can turn anonymous analytics off in Settings at any time.

Purchases and subscriptions

Premium purchases and subscription management are handled by Apple through StoreKit and the App Store.

We do not receive or store your payment card number, bank details, or Apple ID payment credentials. The app stores only whether Premium access is active so that it can unlock Premium features.

Export and sharing

CSV and PDF exports are generated on your device. Exported files leave the device only when you choose to save, share, send, or upload them through iOS sharing options.

After you share an exported file with another app, service, person, clinic, insurer, or storage provider, their privacy practices apply to that copy.

Third-party services

The app uses Apple services for App Store distribution, StoreKit purchases, and optional Apple Health permission. The app uses TelemetryDeck for optional anonymous analytics.

We do not use advertising SDKs, data broker SDKs, social login SDKs, or employer/insurer data-sharing programs.

Retention and deletion

Local app data stays on your device until you delete records, stop tracking where available, delete the app, or erase the device data. Deleting the app may remove local records from that device.

Turning off anonymous analytics stops future analytics events from being sent. Anonymous analytics events already sent to TelemetryDeck are handled according to TelemetryDeck's privacy and retention practices.

Your choices

You can use the app without an account. You can turn anonymous analytics off in Settings, revoke Apple Health permission in iOS, delete local records inside the app where deletion is available, stop pregnancy tracking where available, export your own data, or delete the app from your device.

For privacy questions, contact martin.yize.sun@gmail.com.

Children

The app is intended for pregnant users, people planning pregnancy, and adult supporters. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Medical disclaimer

The app provides general pregnancy information, organization tools, and Germany-specific process guidance. It does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, emergency support, or professional medical advice.

For medical questions, contact a doctor, midwife, clinic, or qualified healthcare provider. In an emergency, contact local emergency services immediately.
