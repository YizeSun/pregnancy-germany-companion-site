Privacy Policy for Pregnancy Germany Companion

Effective date: June 22, 2026

Overview

Pregnancy Germany Companion is an informational pregnancy planning app. It is designed to help users organize pregnancy-related information and planning steps in Germany.

Medical Disclaimer

The app does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, emergency support, or professional medical advice.

Users should consult a doctor, midwife, clinic, or other qualified healthcare provider for medical concerns. In an emergency, users should contact local emergency services immediately.

Analytics

The app may use anonymous analytics to improve app quality, reliability, and usability.

Analytics are not used to identify users personally. Users can disable anonymous analytics in the app settings.

Subscriptions and Payments

Subscription purchases are handled by Apple via StoreKit.

No payment information is collected or stored by the developer.
